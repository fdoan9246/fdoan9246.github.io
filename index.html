Welcome to
Galahad Gardens

Open for our address.

The Galahad Gardens are non-invasive botanical gardens dedicated to the beauty and protection of natural wildlife. We hope you enjoy your visit with us and learn how to promote the conservation of wildlife.

What we Do

    Here at Galahad Gardens we study and promote the life of plants and animals that are essential and vital to life around Eden. Learn about various plants that are all around you, some may even be in your backyard! Bring your family and friends to witness and enjoy the different services and events we hold every day. Ranging from our butterfly conservatory to fast trains to tour the area and have fun.

How much are the tickets?

For adults: 5$
For children ages 13 - 18: 8$
For ages <13: 4$

What time do you open?

We are open 7AM - 11PM Monday - Friday
and open 24/7 on the weekends.

Is smoking allowed

No, we will beat you and kindly ask you to leave if you do not comply to our rules.

Can we bring our animals (ie. dogs and parrots?)

Yes we do. However, you are responsible for your animal. We are not responsible if your animal is lost or dies.

Do you do birthday party and caterings?

We do serve for large groups of people and serve food if asked. Please file out a group pack here.

Is there a limit on how long we can stay?

No, you may stay as long as you like at Galahad Gardens. As long as you respect our community and follow our rules.
